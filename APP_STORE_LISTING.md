## Description

Padel Players App is the social home of padel.

Built for the way padel is actually played — with friends, regular crews, and your local club — not just for booking a court.

**REAL GROUP COORDINATION**
Everything your WhatsApp group wishes it could do. Run weekly polls, organise matches across multiple time slots, see who's playing when, and stop chasing replies. Replace the chaos with a system built for padel.

**INTELLIGENT MATCH BALANCING**
Before you commit to a match, see if the teams are balanced. Our ranking engine suggests fair pairings based on real skill levels — not just availability. Better games, better rivalries, less awkwardness.

**FLEXIBLE MATCH RECORDING**
Padel isn't always rigid. Court time runs out, sets stay unfinished, sometimes the tie-break didn't end. Most apps force you to lie. We let you record what actually happened — with notes, partial sets, and outcomes you and your opponents agree on.

**TRUSTED ELO RANKINGS**
A career ranking that follows you across every match you've ever played. A separate league points system that resets each season. Self-healing, fair, and adjustable by group admins when needed. A rating system players actually trust.

**LEAGUES, COMPETITIONS & CLUBS**
Run social leagues, organise tournaments, or just hang out with your padel crew. Every format. Every level. One platform. Free for players, designed for clubs.

**COMMUNITY BUILT IN**
Post-match player votes, weekly jerseys, community stats, and gamification that makes recreational padel more fun. Find players near you. Build your reputation. Make the game personal.

**WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT**
We're not Playtomic. Playtomic books courts. We organise everything that happens around the match — the people, the politics, the points, the rivalries. The padel community has been waiting for an app like this.

Join your existing padel group or start a new one. Free for players.
