Delete your Padel Players account

You can permanently delete your Padel Players account and its associated data at any time.

Delete from within the app

1. Open the Padel Players app and sign in.
2. Go to your Profile / Settings.
3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Your account is removed and associated personal data is deleted from our systems.

Can't access the app?

Email support@padelplayersapp.com from the address linked to your account and request deletion. We verify your identity and process it within 30 days.

What is deleted

Your profile, account credentials, match and league history, rankings and other personal data linked to your account. Some records may be retained only where legally required (e.g. transaction records for tax), then permanently deleted. See our Privacy Policy.
